## Part 1: Generational Wealth Core (£150M)

### Structure: Family Investment Company (FIC)

A Family Investment Company provides significant tax advantages:
- Corporation tax at 19-25% vs personal income tax at 45%
- Dividend control (take income only when needed)
- IHT planning through share structure
- Asset protection
- Succession planning built-in

#### FIC Share Structure

| Share Class | Holder | Rights |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| A Shares (Voting) | You | Full control, minimal value |
| B Shares (Growth) | Children/Trusts | Capital growth, no voting |
| C Shares (Income) | Discretionary | Dividend rights as needed |

#### FIC Investment Allocation (£150M)

| Asset Class | Allocation | Amount | Expected Return | Annual Income |
|-------------|------------|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| Global Equity Index Funds | 40% | £60,000,000 | 7% | £4,200,000 |
| UK/Global Bond Funds | 20% | £30,000,000 | 4% | £1,200,000 |
| Commercial Property | 15% | £22,500,000 | 5% | £1,125,000 |
| Private Equity/VC | 10% | £15,000,000 | 12% | £1,800,000 |
| Infrastructure Funds | 10% | £15,000,000 | 6% | £900,000 |
| Cash/Gilts Buffer | 5% | £7,500,000 | 4% | £300,000 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **£150,000,000** | **6.4%** | **£9,525,000** |

**Note:** Returns retained in FIC and taxed at corporation tax rates. Draw down via dividends as needed.

#### IHT Planning within FIC

- **Business Property Relief (BPR):** Invest portion in BPR-qualifying assets (unlisted shares, AIM stocks, trading businesses) - 100% IHT relief after 2 years
- **Freezer Shares:** Your voting shares frozen in value; growth accrues to children's shares
- **Life Insurance:** Whole-of-life policy in trust to cover any residual IHT liability

## Part 3: Tax Structure Summary

### Income Tax Mitigation

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| FIC for investment income | Corp tax 25% vs Income tax 45% |
| Dividend vs salary | Lower effective rate |
| ISA contributions | £20K/year tax-free |
| Pension contributions | Up to £60K/year tax relief |
| VCT investments | 30% income tax relief, tax-free dividends |
| EIS investments | 30% income tax relief, CGT deferral |

### Capital Gains Tax Mitigation

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Main residence relief | Full CGT exemption |
| Annual CGT allowance | £6,000/year (use it or lose it) |
| Bed & ISA | Crystallise gains within ISA wrapper |
| Holdover relief | Defer CGT on gifts to trusts |
| Business Asset Disposal Relief | 10% rate on qualifying disposals |

### Inheritance Tax Mitigation

| Strategy | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| FIC share structure | Freeze estate value, gift growth shares |
| Potentially Exempt Transfers | Gifts IHT-free after 7 years |
| Business Property Relief | 100% relief on qualifying assets |
| Agricultural Property Relief | 100% relief on farming assets |
| Charitable legacies | 36% IHT rate if 10%+ to charity |
| Life insurance in trust | Cover IHT liability outside estate |
